## 4. Transition Predictiveness Study

### Methodology
Bucketed 3,678 microstructure observations by transition score decile. Measured whether higher transition scores correlate with better outcomes.

### Results

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total observations | 3,678 |
| % with transition_score = +1.0 | **42.2%** |
| % with transition_score = -1.0 | **40.8%** |
| Near-zero (|s| < 0.05) | **6.0%** |
| Unique values | 519 |

### Diagnosis
**The transition score is DEGENERATE.** 83% of values are clamped to ±1.0:
- The clamping in `compute_transition()` uses `max(-1.0, min(1.0, score))` which aggressively saturates
- With only 2 meaningful states (+1.0 and -1.0), the score provides NO continuous signal
- Top and bottom deciles both have 100% UP selection — the score cannot distinguish direction
- **All 11 trades had transition_score = 1.0000** — it always fired as "bullish"

**Verdict: Transition score does NOT predict outcomes. It is a binary random variable clamped to extremes, providing zero alpha.**

## 7. Simulator Rewrite Requirements

The V20.1 paper engine must be replaced with the following changes:

### Critical Fixes

1. **Binary settlement only** — close_price must be 0.0 or 1.0, never 0.50
2. **Remove synthetic take-profit** — positions held to expiry, TP is binary resolution
3. **Add spread crossing** — entry at ask price, not mid
4. **Add slippage model** — 0-3 ticks beyond best quote
5. **Add fill failure rate** — 15% of orders rejected
6. **Add reprice risk** — 20% chance of price shift during queue
7. **Fix spread field** — use real bid-ask spread, not UP+DOWN token sum
8. **Fix imbalance field** — compute real bid/ask imbalance, not always 0

### Regime Classifier Fixes

1. **Lower velocity thresholds** — BTC 5m data has velocity ~0.001-0.003, thresholds of 0.0005 miss most action
2. **Use real bid-ask spread** — not token price sum
3. **Fix imbalance computation** — must distinguish bid vs ask depth

### Transition Score Fixes

1. **Remove hard clamping to ±1.0** — use tanh or sigmoid soft-clipping
2. **Accumulate evidence over windows** — not instantaneous saturation
3. **Track score distribution** — detect degenerate states
