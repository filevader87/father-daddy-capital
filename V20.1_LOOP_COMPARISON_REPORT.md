3. **Fill rate 0.09-2.7%** — across all loops, <3% of candidates pass all gates. The bucket filter (0.50-0.60) + confidence > 0.85 + transition > threshold produces very few signals.

4. **Zero wins across all live/paper loops** — 4h: 0/6, 5h: 0/11, 6h microstructure: 0/97. The flat trades (entry=close=0.50) aren't wins, they're settlement at indeterminate outcome.

6. **Transition score is degenerate** — polarized at -1.0/+1.0 with mean 0.023. The gate filters on transition magnitude, but the underlying signal is binary noise in the 0.50 bucket.

7. **Only 1 regime observed** — balanced_rotation across all 6 hours. The regime classifier has no diversity in live data.
